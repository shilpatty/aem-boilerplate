Why choose our car insurance

Saga offers comprehensive car insurance for the over 50s. Which includes the following:

2-year fixed price

Choose Saga Plus and fix the price of your insurance for the next renewal, if nothing changes. T&Cs apply.

Uninsured Driver Promise

We’ll repay your excess, and you’ll keep your no claims discount if you’re in a non-fault accident with an uninsured driver and have their details.

No admin fees

If you need to make changes to your policy mid-term we won't charge you a fee. And with Saga Plus we don't charge cancellation fees, so you're not tied in.

No Claims Discount Protection

If you have four or more years’ No claims discount, we’ll protect it against two fault claims in a three-year period (automatically included for eligible customers who choose Saga Plus or Saga Select).

New for old child car seat cover

We’ll replace child car seats with a brand new one after an accident, even if there’s no apparent damage to it. (Unlimited on Saga Plus, up to £1,000 on Saga Select and up to £750 on Saga Standard)
