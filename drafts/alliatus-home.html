Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join an Alliatus mastermind circle?

Start by filling out a short application. We'll review your profile, match you with a compatible circle, and invite you to your first session. The process usually takes 3-5 business days.

What happens during a typical session?

Each session lasts about 90 minutes. You'll share updates, get structured feedback from your circle, and set actionable goals. Sessions are facilitated by a trained host to keep things focused and productive.

How are circles formed?

We match members based on professional stage, industry, goals, and personality. Our goal is to create circles where everyone can both give and receive meaningful support.

Can I switch circles or pause my membership?

Yes! We understand that life and business evolve. You can request a circle change or pause your membership at any time. We're always here to make sure your experience fits your current needs.

Is there a free trial or refund policy?

We offer a satisfaction guarantee for your first month. If the experience doesn't meet your expectations, we'll work with you to find a solution or provide a full refund.
